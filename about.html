Who We Are ?

Ceros has been the premier destination for watch enthusiasts since 2007, serving as both a trusted resource for collectors and buyers and a platform celebrating the artistry of horology. We connect a global audience with the most fascinating and innovative timepieces of our time. At Ceros, we embrace the diversity of watch lovers, bringing together individuals from all walks of life who share a passion for the craftsmanship, design, and creativity behind these timeless tools. Manifesto

Ceros is dedicated to representing the needs and interests of watch enthusiasts while operating as a for-profit, independent media platform. At Ceros, we value honesty and fairness in our communication, though our team doesn't always act strictly as journalists. Instead, Ceros serves as an editorial platform, offering diverse opinions and insights from our passionate writers. Our goal isn't for you to always agree with our views, but rather to provide clear reasoning behind our perspectives so you can decide whether they resonate with you. We promise to remain loyal to the global community of watch owners and enthusiasts, just like us. At Ceros, we take full responsibility for our actions and are committed to promoting transparency and accountability whenever needed.
